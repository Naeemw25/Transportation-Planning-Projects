Assessing and Enhancing Accessibility Through Big Image Data and AI - Canadian Urban Institute - Toronto, ON
•	Assessed current accessibility barriers for people using mobility devices in Business Improvement Areas (BIAs).
•	Utilized urban datasets, artificial intelligence (AI), and Geographic Information Systems (GIS) to analyze accessibility features and identify gaps along travel paths.
•	Conducted background research on accessibility, practices, and designs. Developed actionable plans and policy recommendations for improving accessibility within selected BIAs, aiming to create more inclusive urban areas.
•	Summarized the applicability of AI and urban datasets for accessibility projects, including an analysis of strengths, weaknesses, and suggestions for improvement.

Deploying Private Resources to Catalyse Safe and Sustainable Transportation Networks - Tenblock - Toronto, ON                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
•	Identified and analyzed opportunities for safe and sustainable transportation networks near five specific Tenblock development projects, reviewing existing City of Toronto policies and guidelines to see if sustainable connections are already planned for those areas.
•	Evaluated and selected a target initiative by developing and applying evaluative criteria (like feasibility, cost, public benefit, and political support) to the identified opportunities.
•	Initiated preliminary advocacy for the chosen target initiative by identifying and engaging with key area stakeholders, City staff, and Councillors to evaluate support, gather information, and determine the best political champion.
•	Developed a Long-Term Advocacy Plan, which will build momentum, strengthen support, identify and outline how to overcome outstanding risks, and include a contact list of relevant stakeholders.

Meaford Moves - Municipality of Meaford - Meaford, ON                                                                                                                                             
•	Conducted a comprehensive accessibility and transit needs assessment for Meaford, focusing on seniors and residents with disabilities.
•	Analyzed demographic, socioeconomic, and mobility data to identify service gaps related to aging, disability, and transportation access.
•	Evaluated existing specialized transit operations, including eligibility, fares, service hours, booking procedures, and policy constraints.
•	Developed a strategic vision and service expansion plan proposing extended operating hours, additional service days, and equitable fare structures. Also, researched and benchmarked comparable transit services across Grey and Bruce Counties to identify best practices and scalable improvements.
•	Proposed policy and program enhancements, including monthly passes, volunteer driver programs, healthcare partnerships, and flexible service models.
•	Produced a professional planning report with data visualizations, comparative tables, and evidence-based recommendations for municipal decision-makers.
